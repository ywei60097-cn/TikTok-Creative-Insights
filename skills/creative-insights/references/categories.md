# Category Taxonomy

Use these categories by default. Keep labels stable across daily reports so trends can be compared over time.

## 家电

Products: small appliances, cleaning appliances, kitchen appliances, personal care devices, smart home devices.

Useful keywords: 家电, 小家电, 厨房电器, 清洁电器, 扫地机, 洗地机, 空气炸锅, 咖啡机, 吹风机, 智能家居, 产品展示, 使用前后对比.

Creative patterns to notice: problem-solution demos, exaggerated before/after, invisible feature visualization, scenario montage, family-use vignettes.

## 摄影

Products: cameras, lenses, lighting, phone photography accessories, tripods, gimbals, creator kits.

Useful keywords: 摄影器材, 相机, 镜头, 补光灯, 稳定器, 三脚架, 手机摄影, 开箱, 样片, 运镜.

Creative patterns to notice: side-by-side sample footage, studio-to-outdoor transition, creator POV, cinematic unboxing, lighting transformation.

## 无人机

Products: drones, action cameras, aerial accessories, smart follow devices.

Useful keywords: 无人机, 航拍, 跟拍, 运动相机, 户外, 旅行, 智能避障, 一镜到底.

Creative patterns to notice: impossible camera moves, scale reveal, travel montage, object tracking, AI-generated environment extension.

## 食品

Products: packaged food, snacks, drinks, meal kits, supplements, fresh food.

Useful keywords: 食品, 零食, 饮料, 预制菜, 营养, 低卡, 试吃, 爆浆, 拉丝, 开袋即食.

Creative patterns to notice: texture macro shots, appetite loops, first-person tasting, ingredient explosion, fast recipe transformation.

## 服饰

Products: apparel, shoes, bags, accessories, outdoor wear, shapewear.

Useful keywords: 服饰, 穿搭, 鞋, 包, 防晒衣, 冲锋衣, 显瘦, 面料, 上身效果, 换装.

Creative patterns to notice: instant outfit transition, body-type comparison, fabric close-up, weather stress test, occasion-based styling.

## 美妆个护

Products: skincare, makeup, haircare, fragrance, beauty devices.

Useful keywords: 美妆, 护肤, 彩妆, 香水, 个护, 口红, 粉底, 洗护, 成分, 妆前妆后.

Creative patterns to notice: skin close-up, routine compression, ingredient visualization, before/after proof, sensory packaging shots.

## 家居生活

Products: storage, furniture, bedding, decor, cleaning supplies, bathroom products.

Useful keywords: 家居, 收纳, 床品, 家具, 清洁, 卫浴, 居家好物, 空间改造.

Creative patterns to notice: messy-to-clean transformation, room makeover, hidden storage reveal, tactile close-up, lifestyle scenario.

## 宠物

Products: pet food, toys, smart feeders, litter, grooming products.

Useful keywords: 宠物, 猫, 狗, 宠物食品, 猫砂, 喂食器, 玩具, 除毛.

Creative patterns to notice: pet reaction, owner pain point, mess cleanup, cuteness hook, product POV from pet height.

## 数码产品

Products: phones, headphones, tablets, wearables, chargers, keyboards, monitors.

Useful keywords: 数码, 手机, 耳机, 平板, 键盘, 充电器, 显示器, 智能穿戴, 参数可视化.

Creative patterns to notice: feature visualization, speed tests, desk setup, unboxing rhythm, animated spec overlays.
