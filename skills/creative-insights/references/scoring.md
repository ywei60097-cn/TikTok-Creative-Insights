# Candidate Scoring

Score candidates quickly; do not overfit when public metrics are unavailable.

## Required Filters

- Has a usable URL or discoverable source page.
- Is about a product, ecommerce selling point, product demo, product review, or product-style creative.
- Contains or discusses AI-generated, AI-assisted, virtual production, digital human, synthetic scene, automated editing, or a style directly reusable for AI ecommerce generation.

Reject generic AI news, pure tool tutorials without product creative, and posts with no attributable source.

## 10-Point Score

- Freshness, 0-2: posted or discussed in the requested window; recent enough to matter.
- Product relevance, 0-2: clear ecommerce product and selling point.
- Creative transferability, 0-2: easy to adapt into an AI-generated video workflow.
- Evidence strength, 0-2: direct link, visible creator/title, public metrics or credible repost context.
- Novelty, 0-2: hook, visual style, editing pattern, or category angle is meaningfully distinctive.

Prioritize candidates scoring 7+.

## Pattern Tags

Use 1-3 tags per candidate:

- 痛点开场
- 使用前后对比
- 功能可视化
- 质感微距
- 场景化种草
- 数字人讲解
- AI场景扩展
- 一镜到底
- 开箱节奏
- 反差转场
- 试吃/试用反应
- 参数动画
- 生活方式短剧

## Red Flags

- The same video appears in multiple reposts with no original source.
- Claims of virality are not backed by visible engagement or credible ranking context.
- The content is impressive visually but has no product-selling mechanism.
- The link points to a search page rather than the video or source post.
